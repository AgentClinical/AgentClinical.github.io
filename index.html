Frequently Asked Questions

Will patients know they're talking to an AI?

The AI identifies itself transparently, but conversations feel natural. Most patients appreciate the instant response and zero hold time. We comply with all disclosure requirements.

What happens if the AI can't handle a call?

It seamlessly transfers to your staff or takes a detailed message. You set the escalation rules — the AI follows them precisely.

Does it integrate with my practice management software?

Yes. We support Best Practice, Medical Director, Cliniko, Genie Solutions, and most popular Australian PMS platforms. Custom integrations are available on the Multi-Location plan.

How is patient data protected?

All data is encrypted in transit and at rest. We maintain HIPAA compliance, sign BAAs, and host on Australian infrastructure. Full audit logs are available from your dashboard.

Can I customise what the AI says?

Absolutely. You control the greeting, tone, FAQ responses, booking rules, and escalation protocols. Changes take effect immediately.

What if I want to cancel?

No lock-in contracts. Cancel anytime with 30 days notice. We'll help you transition smoothly.
